Dugasta Properties Development is a promising and evolving developer from Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Over more than 30+ years in the real estate industry, the Chairman and Founder of Dugasta Properties Development, Mr. Tauseef Khan is an expert in the Real Estate Market. Backed by the longstanding success of a prolific portfolio, Dugasta Properties Development is working towards providing its clients with high-quality and value-for-money projects. The company’s unique approach to business involves centering each development’s appeal on customer interests. Whether this means a plethora of investment options or easy payment plans, Dugasta has a wide umbrella of services to offer to property buyers.

As Dubai’s real estate market develops in line with international markets, consumers have come to expect higher value propositions from their choice of properties. Dugasta is ambitious, bold, and innovative. Combined with our commitment to placing customer interests first, these values will take us far in creating a legacy of excellence and trust.

In every aspect, Dubai is living an outstanding story of success written by the vision and ambitions of the visionaries of the country. It has transformed into an international model city with sophisticated landmarks that have impressed the entire world. In this truly 21st-century city, we are aiming to become a “Developer of Choice” for investors worldwide by offering benefits and services which are attractive and totally out-of-box.
